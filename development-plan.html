Development Phases

The project will be developed in three phases over the next two years.

Phase 1: The first phase of the project will convert existing groomed polo fields into eight large to 12 small soccer and lacrosse fields, available for recreational and club youth sports and special events.

Phase 2: A second phase will include the installation of a multi-use dome, which would allow for sports training as well as year-round sports and events. The dome is planned for approximately 80,000-100,000 square feet and be placed on the property in a location allowing for minimal visibility from the highway, preserving a view of open green space.

Phase 3:
A third phase will include the construction of a baseball/softball complex. Eight turf fields will be convertible for full-size baseball to youth-size baseball and softball. The complex would be available to host both weekend and week-long tournaments.
