Location

Where We Are

Blue Sky Sports Complex is conveniently located on 162 Ohaire Road, in the Town of Wallkill, just a short, 8-minute drive from the intersection of Route 17 and U.S. 84 and 20 minutes from New York Stewart International Airport in New Windsor.

With its central tri-state location in Orange County, New York, visitors will easily travel from surrounding areas in New York, New Jersey, Pennsylvania, Connecticut and beyond to Blue Sky Sports Complex for its outstanding athletic and event facilities.

Plus, there’s so much nearby, you can make a vacation out-of-it!
