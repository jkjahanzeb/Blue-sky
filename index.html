THINKING ABOUT HOSTING YOUR EVENT AT BLUE SKY? LEARN MORE

Blue Sky Sports Complex is a brand-new destination venue in the heart of Orange County, NY, and is ready to book a limited number of major community events and sports tournaments this year. The 249-acre property is a distinctive, centrally located site in the Hudson Valley that offers both a picturesque setting that will attract visitors and an expansive location that can accommodate larger numbers of people.

LEARN MORE ABOUT BOOKING AN EVENT

Blue Sky Sports Complex is ideal for festivals and other major gatherings because of the location’s ability to draw thousands of visitors from around the Hudson Valley, Catskills and northern New Jersey region. The property has ample space for parking on flat, open areas. Blue Sky is also a perfect locale for both community recreational and regional travel club soccer and lacrosse tournaments. The facility is home to eight to 12 new, natural grass soccer and lacrosse fields.
